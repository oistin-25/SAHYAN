The Origin of Sahyan

Born from the misty peaks of the Sahyadri mountains, we bring you nature's purest luxury.

A Journey Since 2020

Founded on the philosophy of absolute harmony with nature, SAHYAN began its voyage in 2020. Our founders, driven by an unyielding reverence for the land, set out to cultivate a brand that elevates everyday staples into profound experiences.

We believe that true luxury lies in simplicity and purity, uncompromised by modern shortcuts.

Sourced from Wayanad

The misty altitudes and rich soils of Wayanad dictate the unparalleled caliber of our yield. Every leaf, bean, and root is nourished by monsoons and touched by generations of agricultural wisdom.

Our organic farming philosophy explicitly rejects synthetics, honoring the wild biodiversity of the region.
